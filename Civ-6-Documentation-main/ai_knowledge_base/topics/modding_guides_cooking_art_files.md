# Cooking Art Files

### Getting Art in the Game

1. - Open up the AssetCloud.

- Ensure that your Mod Project is selected.

- Right-Click on AssetCloud.
2. Select `Cook Local Assets`

- Click on `All Files...`

- Launch `Sid Meier's Civilization VI`.

- Press the *Additional Content* button.

- Ensure that your mod is enabled.  If it is enabled, it will have a check-mark next to it.

- Play the game, and enjoy!
